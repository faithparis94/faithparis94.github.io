About Pixel Tech Clinic

Our mission is simple: To eliminate tech downtime and keep you connected to what matters most.

Founded with a vision to provide high-quality electronic repairs, **Pixel Tech Clinic** has grown into a trusted repair hub for students, professionals, and small business owners. We combine technical expertise with a customer-first approach to ensure every repair is handled with precision.

Our Core Services

📱

Phone & Laptop Repairs

We specialize in hardware fixes including screen replacements, battery swaps, and intricate motherboard repairs for all major brands.

💾

Advanced Data Recovery

Accidentally deleted a file? Hard drive crashed? We use specialized software to retrieve your critical business data and personal photos.

🛡️

Virus Removal & Security

We deep-clean your systems of malware, install robust security software, and optimize your OS for maximum speed and safety.

🏢

Maintenance Contracts

For small businesses, we offer routine maintenance visits to ensure your office tech stays healthy and avoids costly breakdowns.
